About Us

Dar Al Zain General Trading LLC, established in 1996, is a distinguished leader in wholesale and retail trading. Located in Dubai, UAE, we offer a wide range of products including food, non-food items, perfumes, cosmetics, garments, and fresh produce. With a strong presence in the GCC market, we’ve expanded into India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Singapore, and the UK.

Our mission is to provide superior products and exceptional service to foster long-term partnerships. We envision becoming a globally recognized leader in the trading industry.
