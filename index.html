About Us

Founded in 1996, Dar Al Zain General Trading LLC has built a reputation as a leader in the wholesale and retail trading industry. Based in Dubai, UAE, we offer an extensive range of products, including food, non-food items, perfumes, cosmetics, garments, and fresh produce.

With a strong presence in the GCC region, we have expanded our operations to India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Singapore, and the UK. Our mission is to provide high-quality products while fostering long-term business relationships.

Al Dhait Market

Always Fresh

Located in Dhait South, Ras Al Khaimah, Al Dhait Market is dedicated to providing a wide selection of fresh produce, groceries, and daily essentials to our community. Known for our commitment to quality and affordability, we ensure that every product on our shelves meets the high standards our customers expect. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to assist you, ensuring a pleasant shopping experience every time you visit.

At Al Dhait Market, we believe in sourcing locally and supporting the community. Whether you are shopping for fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy, or household items, you can count on us for freshness and quality. We are passionate about serving our customers and continually strive to bring the best to Dhait South and beyond.
